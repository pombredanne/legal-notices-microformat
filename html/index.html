Advertisement for Bids

Legal Notice

Sealed bids for the furnishing of all labor, material, equipment, and services for the Bridge Rehabilitation on Cottonport Road over Goodfield Creek in Meigs County, Tennessee will be received until 10:00 A.M. Eastern Time, December 15, 2010 at the Purchase & Finance Office, Courthouse, 17214 State Highway 58 North, Decatur, Tennessee 37322 at which time and place they will be publicly opened and read aloud. Bids being mailed for this work should be addressed to the Meigs County Purchase & Finance Office, P.O. Box 38, Decatur, Tennessee 37322.

The work to be bid will be the Bridge Rehabilitation on Cottonport Road over Goodfield Creek. The work shall include partial and full depth bridge deck repairs, expansion joint removal and replacement, concrete railing modifications, scour improvements, guardrail replacement, repainting existing steel superstructure including containment and disposal of waste, and traffic control. The Contractor shall be responsible for performing the work in compliance with all permits received by the Meigs County Highway Department. Plans, Specifications, and Contract Documents may be obtained from Professional Engineering Services, 7A Cook Street, Sparta, Tennessee, 38583, upon receipt of payment of $100.00 per set (non-refundable). Bids shall be submitted on the separate copy of the PROPOSAL supplied for this purpose. Plans, Specifications, and Contract Documents must be purchased from PES prior to the bid opening for a bid to be considered qualified.
